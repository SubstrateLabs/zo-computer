If you opt in to receive SMS messages from Zo, we may collect the following personal information:

- Your mobile phone number
- Any information you provide in your message responses

Zo does not sell or share your SMS opt-in data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying “STOP” to any message. After you opt out, you will no longer receive text messages from us unless you opt in again.

To request access to or deletion of your SMS-related data, please contact us at support@zocomputer.com.
