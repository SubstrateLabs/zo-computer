Your Zo subscription comes with an always-on cloud computer and monthly AI credits. Usage beyond your monthly AI credit allotment is charged to you at-cost, using the same rates we pay our underlying AI model providers. 

|             | Free                 |  Basic                      | Pro                        | Ultra              |
| --------------- | -------------------- | -------------------------- | ---------------------- | ----------------------- |
| All AI models   | Limited model selection | Included <Icon icon="check"/> | Included <Icon icon="check"/> | Included <Icon icon="check"/> |
| AI credits         | None, limited daily usage | Monthly AI credits | 4X more credits | 10X more credits |
| Always-on computer | Shuts down when inactive | Included <Icon icon="check"/> | Included <Icon icon="check"/> | Included <Icon icon="check"/> |
| Hosted services | Limited | Up to 5  | Up to 10  | Up to 50 |
| Memory | Limited | Up to 32GB | Up to 128GB | Up to 512GB |
| CPU | Limited | Up to 4 cores | Up to 16 cores | Up to 64 cores |
| Fast disk storage | Limited | Up to 128GB | Up to 256GB | Up to 1TB |
| Unlimited network storage | Limited | Unlimited <Icon icon="check"/> | Unlimited <Icon icon="check"/> | Unlimited <Icon icon="check"/> |

We provide you with direct access to AI models without any artificial constraints or limitations. Unlike other tools that mediate your interaction with these models to fit their subscription economics, we practice fair, transparent AI pricing.

<Info>
You can manage your subscription and view your upcoming invoice inside your Zo on the Billing page, accessible from the top of the Settings page.
</Info>
