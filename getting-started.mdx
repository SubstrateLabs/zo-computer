## 3) Set Up Your Zo Greeting & Say Hi

Next up\! It's time to customize your Zo greeting - this is how Zo will great or prompt you when you arrive to your workspace

Click the "home" icon, set your greeting and click save.

![Screenshot 2025-08-25 at 4.02.00 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-08-25at4.02.00PM.png)

Click the setting icon in the upper right, craft your greeting, save, and then click the hand emoji.

Some ideas for how to Zo can greet you:

- top ten news headlines for the day
- work prompt \+ reminder to take a deep breath
- greet you with a joke or a poem

You're all set\!
