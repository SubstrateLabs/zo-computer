## 2) Personalize your Zo in Settings

Go to **Settings** in the botton left corner.

**Choose your theme, chat model, and image model.**

- Zo's chat models include the latest models from GPT, Gemini, Opus, Grok, DeepSeek, and more

**Add rules for how you want your Zo to behave in certain conditions**

- These conditions can include how concise Zo should be in chat or automated work tasks like file creation.

![Screenshot 2025-08-26 at 10.57.16 AM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-08-26at10.57.16AM.png)

**Create "Zofiles," personas for specific occasions to enhance work or just for fun.**

- These personas can be toggled on or off

![Screenshot 2025-08-26 at 10.54.21 AM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-08-26at10.54.21AM.png)

**Integrate your files from Notion and Google Drive**

**Connect your Google Calendar.**

**Connect your email & text**

\*\*Connect your \*\*Enable texting and emailing Zo by adding your phone number and email.

You'll also find some Developer and Advanced settings where you can set your time zone.

![Screenshot 2025-08-25 at 3.40.00 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-08-25at3.40.00PM.png)

## 3) Set Up Your Zo Greeting & Say Hi

Next up\! It's time to customize your Zo greeting - this is how Zo will great or prompt you when you arrive to your workspace

Click the "home" icon, set your greeting and click save.

![Screenshot 2025-08-25 at 4.02.00 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-08-25at4.02.00PM.png)

Click the setting icon in the upper right, craft your greeting, save, and then click the hand emoji.

Some ideas for how to Zo can greet you:

- top ten news headlines for the day
- work prompt \+ reminder to take a deep breath
- greet you with a joke or a poem

You're all set\!
