## Zoverview

Welcome to this step-by-step guide on how to set up and start using your Zo computer.

## Step 0: Sign into your Zo 

Go to \<handle.zo.computer\> on your browser of choice or downlaod our desktop app.

## Section 1) Settings: Personalizing Your Zo Computer

Go to Settings in the botton left hand corner and choose your theme:

![Screenshot 2025-08-25 at 3.20.48 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-08-25at3.20.48PM.png)

Next, add rules for how you want your Zo to behave in certain conditions,

![Screenshot 2025-08-25 at 3.34.16 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-08-25at3.34.16PM.png)

Create your Zo's persona(s) in Zofiles.

![Screenshot 2025-08-25 at 3.34.28 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-08-25at3.34.28PM.png)

Scroll down within your settings to connect your Notion, Google Calendar and Good Drive. Enable texting and emailing Zo by adding your phone number and email.

![Screenshot 2025-08-25 at 3.23.16 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-08-25at3.23.16PM.png)

You'll also find some Developer and Advanced settings where you can set your time zone.
