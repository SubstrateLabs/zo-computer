Welcome to this step-by-step guide on how to set up and start using your Zo computer.

Choose any AI model

Customize your AI with personas, modes, and rules

## Reserve your handle

If you haven't already, click here and choose your handle.

## 2) Sign into your Zo

Go to \<handle.zo.computer\> on your browser of choice or download our desktop app.

## 2) Make Zo Yours

It's time to personalize your Zo.

Go to **Settings** in the botton left hand corner and choose your theme:

![Screenshot 2025-08-25 at 3.20.48 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-08-25at3.20.48PM.png)

Next, add rules for how you want your Zo to behave in certain conditions,

![Screenshot 2025-08-25 at 3.34.16 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-08-25at3.34.16PM.png)

Create your Zo's persona(s) in Zofiles.

![Screenshot 2025-08-25 at 3.34.28 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-08-25at3.34.28PM.png)

Connect your Notion, Google Calendar and Good Drive. Enable texting and emailing Zo by adding your phone number and email.

![Screenshot 2025-08-25 at 3.23.16 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-08-25at3.23.16PM.png)

You'll also find some Developer and Advanced settings where you can set your time zone.

![Screenshot 2025-08-25 at 3.40.00 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-08-25at3.40.00PM.png)

## 3) Set Up Your Zo Greeting & Say Hi

Next up\! It's time to customize your Zo greeting - this is how Zo will great or prompt you when you arrive to your workspace

Click the "home" icon, set your greeting and click save.

![Screenshot 2025-08-25 at 4.02.00 PM.png](/images/Screenshot2025-08-25at4.02.00PM.png)

Click the setting icon in the upper right, craft your greeting, save, and then click the hand emoji.

Some ideas for how to Zo can greet you:

- top ten news headlines for the day
- work prompt \+ reminder to take a deep breath
- greet you with a joke or a poem

You're all set\!
